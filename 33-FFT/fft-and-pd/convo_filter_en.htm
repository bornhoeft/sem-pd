10. Convolution-Filter


After all it is possible to build all various filters, working in the frequency domain. In this kind of filter one signal plays through the body of another. [4.fft.examples-08.convobros.pd]

Fig. 24
Convolution-Filter
 

The signal at the left inlet is like a filter. Only its amplitudes are used for the resynthesis. At the right inlet there is a signal which is like "acting", it is inverted 1/amplitude, and also clipped (by a scalable value). So that all "unimportant" frequencies raise in importance, which leads to a richer spektrum, and this spectrum squeezes through the filter/body, which changes the sound.

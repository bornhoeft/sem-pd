11. Randomgenerated Phases


Another possibility to generate long-playing, non-looping sounds is to genarete phases randomly. The time-structure of the sound is taken away, because the time flow is changed whenever phases are changed in the frequency domain. You receive the impression that all frequencies are played simultaneously. (Fig. 25).

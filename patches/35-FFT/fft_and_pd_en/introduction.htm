1. Introduction

First I apologize for my bad English, I am thankful for every help or hint on mistakes... (marius.schebella@chello.at) 

This article is part of my graduation-work for the course "Computermusic and Electronic Media" at the University of Music and Performing Arts in Vienna/Austria. I describe Signal-Processing-Techniques, I was using for compositions, done with Pd, the real-time graphical programming environment for audio and graphical processing of  Miller Puckette.

I focus mainly on DSP-Techniques involving Fourier-Transformations, describing problems which I had to deal with and which I tried to work off and write together, eventually to give new aspects of viewing or understanding.

let´s go.
